Privacy Policy

Last updated:

InnerMirror is designed to keep your data on-device whenever possible. We do not sell your personal data. The app may store information locally on your device to provide features like the Secrets Vault and life logging.

Data We Collect

  • On-device data you create (e.g., secrets, logs) stored locally.
  • Optional integrations (e.g., HealthKit) only with your explicit permission.

How We Use Data

  • Provide and improve app functionality.
  • Generate insights and “Future You” messages.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties for marketing. Platform services (e.g., iOS) may process data per their policies.

Your Choices

  • You can revoke permissions (e.g., Health) in system settings.
  • You can delete your local data via the app (e.g., burn secrets).

Privacy Policy

100% on-device. No servers. No uploads. Ever. Your data never leaves your phone. Not even we can see it.

Data Collection

We collect nothing. Zero. Nada.

InnerMirror runs entirely on your device. All AI processing, all data storage, all voice generation happens locally on your phone. Nothing is transmitted to any server, ever.

Data Storage

All your data—messages, photos, health data, journal entries, secrets—is stored locally on your device using industry-standard encryption.

Your secrets vault uses SQLCipher encryption. Your life log is stored in encrypted JSON files. Your voice model is stored locally.

We cannot access your data. We cannot see your data. We have no servers to store it on.

Third-Party Services

We use zero third-party analytics. Zero tracking. Zero advertising networks.

The only external connection is for downloading the initial AI model from Hugging Face (a public, open-source model repository). After download, everything runs offline.

Permissions

InnerMirror requests permissions to:

  • Read messages (to understand your communication patterns)
  • Access photos (to analyze your visual life)
  • Access health data (to understand your physical patterns)
  • Access location (to understand where you create and live)
  • Use microphone (for voice journaling and voice cloning)
  • Use biometrics (to lock your secrets vault)

All of this data stays on your device. None of it is transmitted anywhere.

Your Rights

You own your data. You can delete it at any time by uninstalling the app. Your secrets vault has a "Burn Day" feature that permanently deletes everything on December 31st.

Since we don't collect or store your data, there's nothing for us to share, sell, or delete on our end.

Children's Privacy

InnerMirror is rated 17+ and is not intended for children. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy (we won't—we still won't collect data), we'll update this page. But since we don't collect data, there's nothing to change.

Contact

Questions? Contact us at privacy@innermirror.app

But remember: we can't see your data, so we can't help you recover it if you lose your phone. That's the price of complete privacy.
